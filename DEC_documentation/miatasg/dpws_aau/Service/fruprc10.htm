Speaker Replacement

To replace the speaker, shut down the system, remove power, remove the system cover, refer to the figure, and perform the following procedure:

  1. Put on your ESD wriststrap.
  2. Remove the front bezel.
  3. Remove the fan/speaker housing.
  4. Remove the speaker from the housing.
  5. Disconnect the harness from the riser card.
  6. Remove the control panel.
  7. Remove the harness.
