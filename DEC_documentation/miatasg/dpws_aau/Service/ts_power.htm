Power Troubleshooting Checklist

The 300-Watt power supply provides six regulated outputs to the system: +3.3V, +5.0V, +12.0V, -5.0V, -12.0V, and +5VSB (See power pinouts). An output signal, POK_H, is asserted when the +5V output exceeds an undervoltage threshold. A DC remote ON/OFF signal pin is used to enable or disable all the outputs except +5VSB. The power supply contains one 80-mm DC fan.

NOTE: When powering off the system, ensure that it remains off for at least 45 seconds before you reapply power.
