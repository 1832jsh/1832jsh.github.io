ISA DMA Configuration

The floppy interface in the SuperI/O chip is hardwired to DMA channel 2. The parallel interface is allowed to use DMA channel 3 during certain modes of operation. The audio controller chip (ES1888) can use DMA channels 0, 1, or 3 for general DMA and channel 5 for system DMA.

DMA channels 0-3 and 5-7 can be used by any of the three ISA slots on the riser card. The following table summarizes the DMA channel assignments (H=hardwired, P=program selected):

Device DMA Channels
0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
FDC - - H - - - - -
Parallel - - - H - - - -
Audio P P - P - H - -
ISA P P P P P P P P
