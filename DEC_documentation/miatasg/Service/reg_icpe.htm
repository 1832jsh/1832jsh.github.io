Icache Parity Error Status Register (IC_PERR_STAT)
Address: IPR Ibox, Index=11A
The Icache parity error status (ICPERR_STAT) register is a read/write register. The Icache parity error status bits may be cleared by writing a 1 to the appropriate bits.
TMR<13>RWTimeout reset error.
TPE<12>RWTag parity error.
DPE<11>RWData parity error.
