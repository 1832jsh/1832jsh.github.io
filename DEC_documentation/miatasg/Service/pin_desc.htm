Speaker Out Connector

3.5mm phone jack connector (stereo). When no plug is present here and at the side headphone jack, the sound is routed to the internal speaker.

Audio In Connector

3.5mm phone jack connector (stereo). Connects an external stereo source to the ES1888 audio controller. When no plug is present, the LineL and LineR signals are grounded.
